\subsection{Detailed Description}
Definition of \hyperlink{class_subject}{Subject} abstract class. 

A subject is the abstract version of a Model in the M\+VC pattern. A subject has specific state data, in addition to an \hyperlink{class_observer}{Observer} associated with it. When a \hyperlink{class_subject}{Subject} updates its state, it notifies its \hyperlink{class_observer}{Observer} that a change has been made.
